Wrapped LEO
Gateway between LEO and ETH.
Wrapped LEO (wLEO) is an Ethereum ERC20 token that represents LEO token. Each wLEO is backed by real LEO and can be converted back to LEO at any time.
To receive wLEO, send LEO to @wrapped-leo with your Ethereum address as a memo.
To receive LEO, enter your Hive username into "wLEO to LEO" form (front page). You will receive deposit details, so send any amount of wLEO to the given address. Be aware that the address will expire in 7 days, so you will need to generate a new address every week.
To reduce page loading speed, our data is calculated once per hour and might not be 100% up to date.
